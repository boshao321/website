About

Bo Shao / 2024-11-01

I was born in Wuwei, a small city in Gansu Province, Northwestern China, known for its historical significance as an important city on the Silk Road. I earned my bachelor’s degree in Logistics Engineering from Fuzhou University, where I met teachers and friends who had a significant impact on my life. Currently, I am pursuing my Master’s degree in Industrial & Systems Engineering at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, located in Madison, a charming and picturesque town in the heart of the American Midwest. I enjoy trying new things and constantly seek opportunities to challenge myself and grow. Growing up as the only child in my family, I learned to be independent and make my own decisions. Here, I plan to share my random thoughts, experiences, and reading notes.
